Half Day Trail

The half day trail offers up to 4 hours worth of fun, travelling roughly 7 1/2 miles. Perfect for families or friends.

Full Day Trail

The full day trail offers a 12 hour rafting experience for committed or experienced rafters, travelling 24 miles.

Two Day Trail

The Two Day Trail includes 8 hours of rafting on day one, overnight camping gear and location, and 8 hours of rafting on day two. This trail travels a total of 32 miles.
